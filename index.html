Biography

I have been at SenseTime since August 2019, where I currently serve as a senior algorithm researcher and lead a team focused on the advancement and application of universal multimodal embedding and retrieval(UME) to real-world applications. Previously, I specialized in representation learning, implementing it in practical applications across various industries.

Before that, I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science from the Computer Games and Intelligence (CGI) Lab at National Chiao Tung University in June 2019, where my research focused on combining deep reinforcement learning with Computer Game AI (e.g., 2048 Game AI, Chinese Chess AI, AlphaGo/AlphaZero), under the supervision of Professor I-Chen Wu.

I am originally from Fujian, China, a land of rolling hills and mountains, known for its pristine natural beauty and fresh, clean air.
